# Coffee-Shop-Sales

**Conclusion – Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard**

The analysis shows that the coffee shop is performing steadily with total sales of ~$698K from ~149K customers, indicating strong and consistent demand. Customer spending behavior is stable, with an average bill per person of 4.69 and 1.44 items per order, suggesting opportunities for upselling and combo offers.

Sales peak during morning hours (around 7–10 AM), confirming that the shop strongly benefits from breakfast and early workday traffic. Weekdays, especially Monday to Friday, record higher order volumes compared to weekends, highlighting office-driven demand.

From a product perspective, coffee-related items dominate sales, with Barista Espresso and brewed beverages leading revenue contribution. Category analysis shows that core beverages contribute the largest share, while add-ons like flavors and bakery items have potential for growth.

Among store locations, Hell’s Kitchen and Astoria generate higher revenue, while Lower Manhattan shows comparatively lower footfall—indicating scope for targeted promotions or operational improvements there.

Order size distribution reveals that Regular and Large sizes together account for the majority of orders, reinforcing customer preference for value-sized beverages.
